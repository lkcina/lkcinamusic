News

This past school year, I had the pleasure of serving as president of Bowling Green State University's new music student organization, Praecepta. I got to work with an increcibly talented executive board to organize and host events to promote the production, development, and performance of new music in Bowling Green. Some of our events included student concerts and a miniature festival featuring music and educational activities with guest composers and performers. It was such a blessing to be a part of the promotion of new music, and I look forward to many more opportunities like this.

Keep up with what Praecepta is doing this coming school year by following them on social media.
